How to play
You can open boxes, and get cubes. Each cube has its own rarity (and price!) and each box has its own cubes. When you get a cube, you may click on it to inspect it, and if you double click on it, you can sell it. A few planned features will give lower-tier cubes more uses ;)
Jackpot!
Jackpot has been added! Deposit items during the jackpot by double clicking on them! The more you deposit, the greater chance you have to win! You can start a jackpot by depositing an item into the empty jackpot. Bots will also start jackpots themselves! (bots items will scale based on your average item value) You can gain an inventory size greater than the cap by playing jackpot, and I am not responsible for any damages caused by 1000 item inventories. WARNING: REFRESHING THE PAGE WHILE YOUR ITEMS ARE IN THE JACKPOT WILL DELETE THEM. THE BOTS MAY USE BOT CUBES IN ORDER TO MATCH YOUR BET. IF THEY DO, SELL THEM OR BET THEM IMMEDIATELY!!! THEY WILL GRADUALLY DISAPPEAR AFTER RELOADING THE PAGE!!!
